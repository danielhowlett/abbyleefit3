FAQ

Questions, answered

Do you offer in-person or virtual training?

Both. In-person sessions are available in [City/Area], and virtual coaching is available anywhere. You get the same individualized program and the same level of attention either way.

Who is personal training for?

Anyone ready to get stronger and feel better — whether you're brand new to training, coming back from injury or surgery, or feeling stuck at a plateau. Abby specializes in Corrective Exercise, so programs are built carefully around your age, health, and any limitations.

What does nutrition coaching include?

An individualized macro plan, lifestyle habit coaching, and weekly or monthly accountability check-ins. It's focused on building sustainable eating and lifestyle habits — not crash diets.

Do workout plans come with nutrition coaching?

No. Nutrition and lifestyle coaching is its own program and doesn't include workout programming. If you want both training and nutrition together, personal training includes nutrition and lifestyle coaching.

How does small group training work?

Small group sessions include all the elements of personal training — individualized attention, corrective focus, coaching — in person, in groups of up to four people, at a discounted rate of $35 per session per person.

How do I get started?

Fill out the short coaching application — it takes about 3 minutes. Abby reviews your goals, fitness level, and any injuries or limitations, then reaches out to talk through the best fit and get your plan started.
